Projects

This page is dedicated to all the projects I was working on during the last few years on the side.

BIMZap

BIMZap is a project we started with my friends aiming to provide digital solutions for architects with special focus on animations and extensions to Revit. My role is to keep the website maintained, keep our databases running, manage outreach and to provide consultation on python related questions for Revit automations

마틴의 주방 (Martin's Kitchen)

마틴의 주방 (Martin's Kitchen) started as a pastime to resolve "quarantine boredom" associated with the 2020 COVID-19 pandemic.

The main purpose behind it was to spend my time on two things I always wanted to learn well: Cooking and the Korean Language. As an accidental third skill, video editing was just another aspect of the project that I had to dig in hard to make everything happen.

On this Youtube channel I aim to share Hungarian and regional home-cooked recipes with a Korean audience. Hopefully it will bring as much joy to everyone watching as it did for me making it.
